# QUICK VIDEO CREATION GUIDE

## FASTEST METHOD - Online Tool

### Step 1: Get Your Photos
Go to your website: https://jewellery-store-hfug.vercel.app/#/
Save these photos:
- Home page hero images
- Product photos from shop page
- Category images

### Step 2: Use Canva (Free & Easy)
1. Go to: https://www.canva.com/
2. Sign up (free)
3. Search: "Jewellery Slideshow"
4. Choose elegant template
5. Upload your photos
6. Arrange in order:
   - Opening: Sparkle/hero image
   - Middle: 5-7 product photos
   - Ending: Your best piece

### Step 3: Customize
- Add "R&S Jewellery" text
- Add "Shop Now" button
- Choose elegant transitions
- Add background music
- Duration: 20 seconds

### Step 4: Export
- Click "Share" > "Download"
- Format: MP4
- Quality: HD (1080p)
- Name: jewellery-hero.mp4

### Step 5: Add to Website
- Move to: public/videos/
- Test: npm run dev
- Push to GitHub when ready
