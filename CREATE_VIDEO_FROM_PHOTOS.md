# CREATE VIDEO FROM YOUR WEBSITE PHOTOS

## Step 1: Get Your Photos
1. Go to: https://jewellery-store-hfug.vercel.app/#/
2. Right-click on jewellery photos
3. Save 5-10 best photos to a folder

## Step 2: Create Video (Free Options)

### Option A: CapCut (Mobile)
1. Download CapCut app (free)
2. Add your jewellery photos
3. Choose "Slideshow" template
4. Add elegant transitions
5. Add background music
6. Export as MP4

### Option B: Canva (Online)
1. Go to canva.com
2. Search "Video Slideshow"
3. Upload your jewellery photos
4. Use elegant template
5. Add music and text
6. Download as MP4

### Option C: DaVinci Resolve (Desktop)
1. Download DaVinci Resolve (free)
2. Create new project
3. Import your photos
4. Add to timeline
5. Add transitions
6. Export as MP4

## Step 3: Settings
- Duration: 20 seconds
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Format: MP4
- Style: Elegant, slow transitions

## Step 4: Save Video
1. Name: jewellery-hero.mp4
2. Place in: public/videos/ folder
3. Test on your website!

## Your Website Photos Include:
- Beautiful chains
- Elegant earrings
- Stunning necklaces
- Professional product shots
- Gold and silver items

## Video Ideas:
- Start with sparkle effect
- Show each product for 2-3 seconds
- End with R&S Jewellery logo
- Add elegant background music
