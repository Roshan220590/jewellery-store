🏦 STEP 1: Choose Payment Gateway
🇮🇳 For Indian Market (Recommended):
Razorpay - Most popular, easy setup, supports UPI, Cards, Net Banking
PayU - Good alternative, competitive rates
Paytm - Popular for mobile users
Cashfree - Good for startups
🌍 For International:
Stripe - Global standard, excellent documentation
PayPal - International customers
Square - Good for US market
💡 My Recommendation: Start with Razorpay - easiest for Indian jewellery business

📋 STEP 2: Get Business Documents Ready
Required Documents:
Business Registration
GST Registration (mandatory for e-commerce)
Shop Act License or Company Incorporation
Bank Account
Current Account (not savings account)
Bank statement (last 6 months)
Identity Documents
PAN Card (Business PAN if registered)
Aadhar Card of authorized signatory
Address Proof
Business address proof (rent agreement/utility bill)
Cancelled cheque

🌐 STEP 3: Create Payment Gateway Account
For Razorpay:
Visit razorpay.com
Click "Sign Up" → "Create Business Account"
Fill business details:
Business name: "R&S Jewellery"
Business type: "E-commerce"
Website URL: "your-website-domain.com"
Complete KYC verification (upload documents)
Get API Keys:
Test Key ID & Test Key Secret (for development)
Live Key ID & Live Key Secret (for production)
