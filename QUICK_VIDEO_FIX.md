# QUICK VIDEO FIX

## Step 1: Download Video
Go to: https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/jewellery/

## Step 2: Find Good Video
Search for: "luxury jewellery" or "gold necklace"

## Step 3: Download
- Click on a beautiful video
- Click "Free Download"
- Choose MP4 format
- Save to your computer

## Step 4: Rename & Place
1. Rename the file to: `jewellery-hero.mp4`
2. Move to: `C:\Users\Roshan_Sawkar\CascadeProjects\jewellery-store\public\videos\`

## Step 5: Test
1. Run: `npm run dev`
2. Check your homepage
3. Video should play!

## Alternative: Use Any Video
Even a phone video of your jewellery will work!
- Record 20 seconds of jewellery
- Save as `jewellery-hero.mp4`
- Place in `/public/videos/` folder
