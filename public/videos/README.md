# Jewellery Showcase Videos

Place your jewellery showcase videos in this directory:

## Recommended Videos:
- `jewellery-showcase.mp4` - Main showcase video
- `jewellery-showcase.webm` - WebM format for better performance

## Video Specifications:
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or higher
- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds (looped)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264) or WebM
- **Content:** Showcase various jewellery types (necklaces, earrings, bangles, rings, etc.)
- **Style:** Elegant, professional lighting, rotating/panning shots

## Alternative: Image Slideshow
If you don't have a video, you can use a series of high-quality images that auto-rotate.

## How to Add:
1. Place your video file in this directory
2. Name it `jewellery-showcase.mp4`
3. The video will automatically play on the homepage

## Tips:
- Keep file size under 10MB for fast loading
- Use muted autoplay for better user experience
- Ensure the video showcases a variety of jewellery pieces
